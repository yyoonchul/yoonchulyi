Most mutations are disadvantageous to an organism's survival and tend to disappear over generations.

However, a very select few, chosen by natural selection, become the driving force of evolution and create new species.

In our society, too, there are those who create new things rather than just inheriting and repeating what already exists.

The vast majority of them face failure. But the few who survive change the world. They create new products, cultures, demands, and standards that never existed before. They are the ones who lead the evolution of the world.

Here, I will share the journey of a story that will either change the world or fade away without a sound.
