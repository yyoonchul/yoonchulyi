Developed a full-stack AI platform that transforms ideas into full-length novel drafts. Architected a 6-step generative pipeline using LangChain and FastAPI, incorporating research from 60+ papers to improve EQ-Bench scores. Conducted on-site user research with writer communities in the SF Bay Area to identify core user needs and refine product direction.
