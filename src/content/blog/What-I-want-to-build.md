title: What I want to build
date: 2025-01-01
---

What I have always thought about is changing the world. To be precise, changing the way people live.

Search engines changed how people consume information to such an extent that a new word, "Googling," was coined. Instagram created a new way for people to form social relationships. The products created by these great companies have redefined our "daily lives" by changing the way we live. Most recently, it seems TikTok and ChatGPT are doing just that.

I have seen numerous success stories of companies, but the ones that truly make my heart race seem to share this common thread.

They created value that people could not enjoy before, and that added value became the norm for the next generation. It seems that change has mostly come with the great waves of technology.

What we often talk about: PC, Internet, Mobile, Cloud, etc.

New technology makes possible what was impossible before and creates value that could not be enjoyed previously. This new way of life, enjoying this added value, becomes the everyday over time, and the world changes.
